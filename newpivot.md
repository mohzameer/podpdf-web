# PodPDF Is Now a Paid Service

**Published:** June 13, 2026

We have moved PodPDF to a paid-only model. This page explains what changed, who is affected, and how to get started.

---

## What Changed

PodPDF no longer offers a free tier. All accounts now require a credit purchase before generating PDFs.

If you signed up before this change, your account has been automatically moved to a **pending** state. You will not lose any of your data, jobs, or settings — you simply need to purchase credits once to reactivate your account and carry on as normal.

---

## How to Activate Your Account

1. Log in to your PodPDF account.
2. Go to **Buy Credits** from the sidebar.
3. Choose a credit package and complete the purchase.
4. Your account upgrades instantly — no waiting, no approval needed.

You can then use the API and the dashboard exactly as before.

---

## Why We Made This Change

Running PodPDF involves real infrastructure costs: compute, storage, and bandwidth for every PDF generated. The free tier attracted a large number of users who never converted to paying customers, which made the service unsustainable.

By moving to a paid model, we can:

- Keep the service fast and reliable for users who depend on it.
- Invest in new features and conversion types.
- Offer responsive support to paying customers.

We believe the pricing is fair — you pay only for what you use, and credits do not expire.

---

## Pricing

PodPDF uses a simple credit model. Each PDF generation deducts a small amount from your credit balance. There are no subscriptions, no monthly minimums, and no surprise charges.

See the [Buy Credits](/buy-credits) page for current package options and per-PDF pricing.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Will my old jobs and history be deleted?**
No. All your previous jobs, API keys, and webhook configurations are preserved.

**Do credits expire?**
No. Credits remain in your account until you use them.

**What happens if I run out of credits?**
API calls will return an error until you top up your balance. You can purchase more credits at any time from the dashboard.

**I had the free plan — do I get any credits for free?**
New accounts start with a zero balance and require a purchase to activate. There are no complimentary starter credits.

**Can I still use my existing API key?**
Yes. Your API key continues to work as soon as your account is active (i.e., after you purchase credits).

**What if I have questions or issues?**
Reach out via the support link in the dashboard. Paying customers receive priority responses.
